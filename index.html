Pin Wheel Sandwich

Ingridients
  • Wheat bread slices
  • Butter
  • Coriander Chutney
  • Roasted Cumin powder
  • Chat Masala
  • Black Pepper
  • Shredded Carrot
  • Boiled Potatoes
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Shredded Spinach
Method

  • Take wheat bread and remove all the edges
  • Take the bread slices and roll it down using rolling pin
  • Butter bread slices and apply Coriander Chutney evenly.
  • Put shredded carrot and boiled potatoes over the bread slice and gently press it down
  • Season with roasted cumin powder, chat masala and Black Pepper
  • Put another layer of bread over the carrot and fill bread slice with shredded cheese
  • Put third layer of bread and add spinach
  • Season with roasted cumin powder, chat masala and Black Pepper
  • Now gently roll with all bread slices together to make roll
  • Take wax paper and roll it around the roll and keep it in refrigerator.
  • After 30 minutes, take out rolls and remove the wax paper
  • Cut them into around half inch sandwiches

Tuppa Dosa

Ingridients
  • 100 Gms - Dhal, Urad (white)
  • 250 g - Maida
  • 4- Medium green chillies
  • Roasted Cumin powder
  • 3-4 - Small onions
  • 1 inch- Ginger
  • Salt to taste.
Method

  • Soak the urad dal in water until soft. Then grind it into a paste, and keep aside for 10-12 hours.
  • Now add the maida, salt to the batter and mix well. Add the finely cut chillies, onions and ginger to the batter.
  • Meanwhile heat oil on a tawa, spread the oil with a brush, and put a ladle full of batter on it. Spread it out with the back of a ladle. Now close the lid for 1 minute.
  • Now open the lid and fry on both sides. Serve hot.
